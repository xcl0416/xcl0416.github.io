About

Research shaped by signals, systems, and experiments.

I am Chenlu Xiang. During my PhD period at Shanghai University, my work focused on wireless systems and mobile computing: robust indoor localization, WiFi-based sensing, CSI data processing, transfer learning for fingerprints, and cooperative localization.
